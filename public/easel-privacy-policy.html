Privacy Policy

Easel AR-Art Studio

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Easel AR-Art Studio is an Android drawing-assist app published by FutureProof Labs. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and what is not collected.

Summary

Easel AR-Art Studio does not require an account and does not collect, store, or sell personal information through the app itself.

Camera Access

Easel uses your device camera so you can view paper or another drawing surface behind a reference overlay. Camera access is used only while the app is running and only to provide the live tracing experience.

Images You Choose

If you select an image to use as a drawing reference, that image is used locally on your device as an overlay. Easel does not upload your images to FutureProof Labs servers.

Data Collection

  • Easel does not create user accounts.
  • Easel does not collect contact details through the app.
  • Easel does not upload camera footage or selected reference images.
  • Easel does not sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

The app may be distributed through Google Play. Google may collect information according to its own policies when you browse, install, or update apps. FutureProof Labs does not control Google Play data practices.

Children

Easel is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data practices change. Updates will be posted at this page.
